Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about withdrawals and fees

❓ Why is the fee separate from my withdrawal amount?▼
The blockchain network requires "gas fees" paid in native coins. These fees go to network validators, not to UNIVERSAL STOCK TRADE.
❓ What happens if my timer expires?▼
Your withdrawal request will be cancelled. Start a new withdrawal.
❓ Why does the fee range from 5% to 15%?▼
Fees vary based on network congestion, bank costs, card fees, and courier distances.
❓ How long does verification take?▼
1-2 business days after submitting TXID and receipt.
❓ Do I need native coins in my wallet?▼
Yes. Without native coins (BTC/ETH/TRX), the blockchain will reject your transaction.
❓ What wallets do you support?▼
Trust Wallet, MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, Ledger, Trezor, and any BTC/ETH/TRC20/BEP20 wallet.

Why does the blockchain charge a fee?

Understanding why fees are separate from your withdrawal amount

1. The Blockchain's "Fuel" Mechanism

The blockchain network requires energy to process any transaction — crypto, bank transfer, card load, or cash mailing. This energy is paid using the native coin (BTC, ETH, TRX, BNB).

  • Your withdrawal value = passenger riding in a car
  • Native coin = gasoline required to move the car

2. Why the Fee Varies (5-15%)

Fees vary based on: network congestion, bank costs, card fees, and courier distances. UNIVERSAL STOCK TRADE passes the exact cost to you — never deducted from your withdrawal.

Bottom line:

No native coin = no transaction, regardless of how much value you're withdrawing.
